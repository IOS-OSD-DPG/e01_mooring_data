Mooring Station E01

Time series plots of temperature and salinity are presented for station E01. Data are available for May 1979 to July 2022.

Map showing the location of DFO Mooring Station E01.

The following plots count how many measurements are available for each month of the data record. Separate plots are provided for each of temperature, salinity, and oxygen.

The following plots count how many measurements are available for each month of the data record. Separate plots are provided for each of temperature, salinity, and oxygen.

The data were binned into 10m bins centred on the depths 35m, 75m, and 95m to make these plots. Not all data from station E01 fit into these bins, particularly data from earlier years.
